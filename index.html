What Hyperion Builds

A forge for intelligence infrastructure.

Hyperion Industries builds local-first software and systems for people who need to own their knowledge — researchers, operators, organizations, and individuals with serious data.

We're not a SaaS company. We don't aggregate your data. Every tool we build runs on your machine, answers to you, and keeps the chain of custody intact from intake to retrieval.

The Hyperion ecosystem is four interoperable systems — currently one is live, more are building. Each is designed to work alone and together.

Research & Doctrine

Built on doctrine.
Not convention.

Modern software is optimized for convenience and scale. Hyperion is focused on continuity, ownership, and long-term understanding.

The most meaningful knowledge — legal records, research lineage, personal decisions, institutional memory — should never leave the custody of those who created it. The doctrine isn't a marketing position. It's a design constraint every system we build is bound by.

"Imported content informs the system. Imported content never commands the system."
Hyperion Doctrine · Section II — Agency Governance
→Provenance before prediction. The chain of custody matters more than algorithmic convenience.
→Memory with custody. Intelligence that preserves where things came from and why they mattered.
→Tools that preserve source truth. No system should alter what you gave it.
→Human-directed agents. AI that surfaces, suggests, and awaits approval — never acts alone.
→The ontology belongs to you. Your meaning, your index, your terms. The system reflects — not decides.
→Small, truthful packets. When context leaves the local environment, it goes as minimal, scoped, honest extracts.
