Why We Build This

Modern software is optimized for convenience and scale. Hyperion is focused on continuity, ownership, and long-term understanding.

Most tools are built for the average workflow. They abstract away provenance, centralize your data, and optimize for retention — not for your ability to understand and control what you've built over time.

We build the opposite. Software designed for people and organizations that cannot afford to lose the thread of their own knowledge.

Local-first
Your data never leaves unless you chooseAll Hyperion tools run on your machine. No accounts required. No passive data collection.
Provenance
Every artifact knows its originWhere it came from, when it arrived, what touched it — preserved immutably from intake.
Oversight
AI that proposes, never acts aloneIntelligence that surfaces, suggests, and waits for your approval. Governance before capability.
Transparency
Open formats, inspectable indexesPlain JSON sidecars. No proprietary lock-in. You can read every file we generate with a text editor.
Continuity
Built to last, not to retainSystems designed to remain useful as your knowledge grows — across years, projects, and tools.
